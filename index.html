My Background

I immigrated from the philippines, and have been living here in Canada for 8 years. I graduated highschool in 2023 where I took a quick year off before entering the graphic design program. I have been using digital softwares such as Photoshop for a while now as It is my main software for drawing along with photo editing. I also have a keen understanding in Illustrator along with Premier Pro. I started as a traditional artist with charcoal pencils then slowly transitioned to digital where I spent most of my time. I mainly use procreate for quick works and sketches.
